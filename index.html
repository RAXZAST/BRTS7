ABOUT BRTS7

BRTS7 / Becker Ltda is an independent label focused on pushing the boundaries of Funk, Phonk, and electronic music.

Founded by Rafael Becker and João Victor Leite, it stands for innovation and raw underground energy. The label has built momentum through standout releases like SYMBIOTE and NERVY FUNK. BRTS7 is dedicated to discovering new talent and shaping the future of independent sound.

OUR SUCCESS

Milestones like SYMBIOTE and Nervy Funk highlight BRTS7’s rise, defining its raw funk-driven sound and growing impact in the underground scene.
